Photo & Video

Youth are provided the opportunity to collaborate with professional videographers and develop a project from beginning to end. This is an especially powerful internship opportunity for young aspiring videographers, writers, and future creatives alike.

Throughout this opportunity, we ensure youth share equitable space with the team and their perspective is equally valued. IVOC core themes within this opportunity are self-identity (who are you), goal setting (to achieve the dream), and professionalism. Connect with us to learn more about this inspiring opportunity.
