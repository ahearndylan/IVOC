Inner Voice Outer Change (IVOC) provides therapeutic services that foster youth empowerment through the creation of music and multimedia projects. Our approach encourages marginalized youth to partner with IVOC as they lean into the therapeutic process of self-expression. These healthy alternatives often prove to be effective in engaging adolescents who struggle with traditional modes of interpersonal communication.








Go upstream with us!

We began our Upstream Initiative to support the mental well-being of our kids before they face serious challenges. We aim to help them develop healthy coping methods early on. With your help, our Upstream Initiative will reward students for academic success by providing FREE access to self-care options. Join us in taking proactive steps to support their well-being!
